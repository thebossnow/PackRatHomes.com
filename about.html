About Us

At Pack Rat Homes, we are a dedicated team focused on providing compassionate property restoration and cleanup services for those dealing with hoarding situations. We understand that hoarding can stem from deep emotional attachments, life transitions, or other personal challenges, and our approach is always rooted in empathy and respect. Our mission is to help individuals and families reclaim their spaces, creating safe and functional environments without judgment. With years of experience in professional cleanup, we blend expertise with genuine care to support our clients through what can be an overwhelming process. What truly drives us is the opportunity to make a positive impact on people's lives. We recognize the sensitivity involved—whether it's privacy concerns, family dynamics, or the emotional weight of letting go—and we tailor our services accordingly. Listening to your unique story allows us to work at your pace, ensuring the experience feels supportive rather than intrusive. We genuinely enjoy helping others transform chaotic spaces into peaceful homes, offering the understanding and resources needed to navigate these challenges with dignity. No project is too large or too small for our team. From light decluttering to comprehensive cleanouts involving biohazards or animal-related issues, we handle everything with professionalism and attention to detail. Our certified experts use safe, efficient methods for removal, sorting, and sanitization, always adapting to fit your specific wants and needs. Whether you need a single room refreshed or an entire property restored, we're here to minimize disruption and maximize results. In addition to our core cleanup services, we offer optional real estate guidance for those interested in exploring next steps with their property. This could include insights on market preparation or connections to potential buyers, but only if it aligns with your goals—we're primarily committed to the cleanup itself. Our aim is to empower you with choices, helping create lasting positive change in your living space and overall well-being. Ready to discuss how we can assist? Contact Pack Rat Homes for a free, no-pressure consultation. We're here when you need us, focused on what matters most to you.
